home

plate of a delicious filipino adobo

ingredients

  • meat(chicken)
  • soysauce
  • vinegar
  • bayleaf
  • peppercorns
  • onions
  • garlic

steps to cook

  1. marinate meat in soysauce and garlic for an hour or overnight
  2. sear the meat in a large pot or wok
  3. add remaining marinade, water, bayleafs, and ppercorns
  4. cook til meat tinder 30mins or 40
  5. add vinegar and boil for 2-3mins
  6. then serve
