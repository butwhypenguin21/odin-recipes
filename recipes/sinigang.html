SINIGANG

home

plate of a filiino sinigang dish

ingredients

  • pork or ribs
  • onions
  • tomatoes
  • knorr cubes or mamasitas
  • daikon radish
  • eggplant
  • okra

steps to cook

  1. boil meat
  2. remove brown foam
  3. add onions, tomatoes then boil 45 mins
  4. add viggies
  5. boil 5-8 mins
  6. then serve
